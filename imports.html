FCL & LCL shipments

ELT Express is able to assist companies and people in accessing new markets and possibilities by offering a dependable and affordable import and export service, resulting in growth and success for all parties.

In order to meet your needs, ELT Express, a well established FCL and LCL player, is prepared to manage a variety of customized requirements. Because of the strength of our network, which serves 2400+ direct tradelanes, our agreements with core carriers allow you move at an ideal transportation time and cost. This decreases the transit time of your shipment and enhances cargo security.  We provide end-to-end logistic services for your cargo using cutting-edge technology, seasoned employees, local knowledge, and value-added services.

ELT Express offers you a number of benefits:

  • Ocean freight solutions based on several carrier choices as needed
  • Transportation time and cost through multiple providers are optimized
  • Transport of bulky, oversize, and brittle objects
  • Worldwide deliveries from door to door
  • Dedicated FCL operations and advisory team
  • End-to-end transit is guaranteed to be safe thanks to inland trucking service and warehouse capabilities.
  • Tradelanes that lower trans shipping costs and time are served by a global network.
  • Also does away with the requirement for various cargo handling
